What is InstaSearch?

InstaSearch is a free, minimalistic image search engine built using the Unsplash API. Whether you’re a designer, content creator, developer, or student – you can find royalty-free, high-resolution images in seconds. Built by Kethan VR, InstaSearch was created to help creatives get inspired without popups, ads, or distractions.

With a lightning-fast UI and mobile-friendly design, InstaSearch makes discovering photos feel effortless. No account needed. Just search, scroll, and save.
